In Conclusion:

To conclude this statistical analysis report, we can say that in terms of the economic value of trade, North America contributed the most. In terms of the weight of the pharmaceutical products, Europes trades have proven to be the heaviest. Africa had the least contributions to the trade, be it through its economic value or its weight. Our analysis gives us reason to believe that the weight of the product being traded does not always have a correlation with the economic value of the product, but instead the economic value is correlated with the type and quality of the product itself. This is clearly shown by the values in kilograms and USD presented by Europe and North America, because as stated, North America had the highest economic value in trade of pharmaceutical products despite the weight of their products being less than the weight of products of a continent like Europe.

While analyzing the data, we found that all of the continents trade values had decreased during 2020. From this, we inferred that a possible cause to this decrease was the Covid-19 virus. This is because when Covid-19 hit the world, in order to protect the public from the virus, countries began isolating their citizens. Due to the isolation of the countries own citizens, countries ended up distancing themselves and closing their doors to other countries too, which meant that trade would be slightly difficult to do, therefore causing the decrease of trade. Though there was still trade because despite the circumstances, trade was and is still an important thing in our world, especially global trade.
